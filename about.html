WELCOME TO
HOLISTIC EDUCATION

"Intelligence plus character-that is the goal of true education." - Martin Luther King Jr.
Every child who becomes part of Gurukula is fondly called a GurukulaMite. We mould our students with care and love who will, proactively, make this world a better place. Our able administrators lead the faculty, students' fraternity and the parents to achieve our motto "Door to the Pinnacle of Success." We are completely academic oriented. We follow a unique system of education which aims at strengthening and fostering the innate and inherent abilities and talents of our students. Skill development is given prime importance. Students' oral and aural skills are frequently assessed. Weekly projects and learning activities nurture the students' curiosity, inquisitiveness and make teaching-learning process 'student centric. Our students are a community of achievers and our incredible achievements flow from a community that nurtures and encourages, with the expectation that the pupils will achieve their best.
